Services offered

Transport of farm produce

The company offers transport of produce from the farms to desired place within the country. The company offers tractors and required loaders of goods from the farm to the place the goods are required. For destinations that are quite far we offer transits and lorries for they are more efficient though more costly compared to the tractors.

Warehouse services

The company provides warehouse services offerring storage for upto three months. Goods get to be kept in a good condition as we have state of the art warehousing facilities.

Transport of farm inputs

We as a company are aimed at making farming better and as a result we are dedicated to enabling farmers to get whichever farm input at their disposal with so much ease.
